About GOK-GAF

The Government of Kenya-Green Army Foundation (GOK-GAF) is a collaborative initiative focused on addressing climate change challenges while creating opportunities for Kenyan youth through environmental conservation and sustainable development projects.

Our Mission

To create sustainable environmental solutions while empowering Kenya's youth through meaningful employment, skills development, and community engagement.

Our Vision

A green, resilient Kenya where young people lead environmental conservation efforts and build sustainable communities.

Climate Resilience Programs

Our comprehensive programs are designed to build resilience against climate change while creating sustainable livelihoods and environmental conservation.

Afforestation

Restoring Kenya's forest cover through large-scale tree planting initiatives led by youth groups across all 47 counties.

Water Resource Management

Implementing sustainable water harvesting techniques and conservation practices to address water scarcity in arid regions.

Renewable Energy

Promoting clean energy solutions including solar installations and energy-efficient cookstoves for communities.

Waste Management

Creating circular economy initiatives for waste reduction, recycling, and upcycling to reduce environmental pollution.

Sustainable Agriculture

Training youth in climate-smart agricultural practices to enhance food security and resilience in farming communities.

Community Adaptation

Supporting vulnerable communities to adapt to changing climate conditions through capacity building and infrastructure development.

Kenya's Green Army

Our flagship youth employment initiative providing opportunities for young Kenyans to engage in environmental conservation while earning sustainable livelihoods.

Youth-Led Climate Action

The Green Army mobilizes young people across Kenya to participate in climate action projects that provide income while building sustainable environmental solutions.

  • Employment opportunities for 50,000+ youth
  • Comprehensive training in environmental management
  • Skill development and entrepreneurship opportunities
  • Gender-balanced participation and inclusion
Join the Green Army

Green Army Growth Timeline

2022

Initial pilot program launched in 5 counties with 5,000 youth

2023

Expansion to 20 counties with 20,000 youth engaged

2024

National rollout across all 47 counties with 50,000 youth

2025

Integration with national climate policies and diversification of green jobs
